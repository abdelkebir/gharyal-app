Here's how it works

The following steps will take place when you apply for financing for your desired watch. We look forward to making your dream watch come true. We are at your disposal for questions of any kind. 

1. Choose a watch

Look for your desired watch in our range and select it.

2. Application for funding

Fill out the checkout form completely. When filling it out, please make sure that you don't forget anything.

3. Confirmation or Rejection

Your application will be reviewed and you will receive an answer within 24 hours as to whether your application for funding has been approved or not.

4. Contractual Documents

After your financing has been confirmed, you will receive the contract documents by e-mail. Please read them carefully. Now all you have to do is sign and send the documents back to us.

5. Right of withdrawal 14 days

From this point in time you have 14 days to withdraw from the contract. During this time we will prepare your product for you.

6. Shipping / Collection

Now it is time. Your watch is now ready for you. You have the choice of whether we should send the watch for you or whether you would like to pick it up from our office.
